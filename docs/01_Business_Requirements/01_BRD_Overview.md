# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Purpose

This Business Requirements Document (BRD) defines the business needs, objectives, stakeholders, and high-level functional expectations for the AILPG platform.

The BRD serves as the bridge between business vision and technical implementation.

It is the primary reference for:

- Product Management
- Business Analysts
- Solution Architects
- Engineering Teams
- QA Teams
- Project Managers

---

# Project Summary

AILPG is an AI-powered platform that converts educational MP4 videos into interactive learning experiences.

Instead of manually creating lessons, quizzes, subtitles, and translations, educators upload a video and the platform automatically generates:

- Speech transcript
- OCR text
- Mathematical formula extraction
- Topic segmentation
- Interactive quizzes
- Explanations
- Notes
- Multi-language translations
- Interactive HTML lessons

Teachers review the AI-generated lesson before publishing it to students.

---

# Business Vision

Enable educators to transform a single educational video into a complete digital learning course with minimal manual effort.

---

# Business Goals

- Reduce manual content creation
- Improve student engagement
- Support multilingual education
- Increase teacher productivity
- Build a scalable SaaS platform
- Enable AI-assisted learning

---

# Business Scope

Included:

- AI lesson generation
- Teacher workflow
- Student portal
- Admin dashboard
- Subscription management
- Analytics

Excluded from Version 1:

- Live classroom
- Video conferencing
- VR/AR learning
- Native desktop application
