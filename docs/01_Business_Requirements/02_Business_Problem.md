# 1. Purpose

This document identifies the core business problems in creating and delivering educational video content.

It provides the justification for investing in the AILPG platform.

---

# 2. Industry Background

Educational institutions increasingly depend on recorded video lessons for:

- Schools
- Colleges
- Coaching Centers
- Online Learning Platforms
- Corporate Training

While video-based education has become common, most learning content remains passive and requires significant manual preparation before it becomes interactive.

---

# 3. Current Challenges

## CP-001 Manual Course Preparation

After recording a lesson, educators often spend additional time creating:

- Notes
- Quizzes
- Assignments
- Subtitles
- Translations
- HTML content

This increases both cost and delivery time.

---

## CP-002 Passive Learning

Students typically:

- Watch videos
- Pause manually
- Take notes independently
- Receive limited feedback

There is little interaction during the lesson.

---

## CP-003 Language Barriers

Educational videos are frequently available in only one language.

Students who prefer another language have fewer learning options.

---

## CP-004 Limited Analytics

Teachers often know:

- who enrolled,
- and who completed a course,

but may lack insight into:

- where students struggled,
- which questions were difficult,
- which lessons were replayed,
- and where learners dropped off.

---

## CP-005 Repetitive Work

Every new course often repeats the same manual workflow:

1. Upload video
2. Write transcript
3. Create subtitles
4. Create quizzes
5. Build lesson pages
6. Publish

Many of these tasks can be partially automated.

---

# 4. Root Cause Analysis

| Problem | Root Cause |
|----------|------------|
| Long publishing time | Manual content creation |
| Passive learning | Lack of interactive lesson features |
| Low scalability | Human-dependent workflows |
| High operational cost | Repeated manual effort |
| Delayed publishing | Multiple disconnected tools |

---

# 5. Business Impact

Without automation:

- Course publication is slower.
- Teachers spend more time on repetitive tasks.
- Students receive less interactive learning.
- Institutions have higher operational costs.
- Scaling content production becomes difficult.

---

# 6. Opportunity

Artificial Intelligence enables a new workflow:

Upload MP4

↓

Automatic analysis

↓

Speech transcription

↓

OCR extraction

↓

Formula recognition

↓

Topic segmentation

↓

Quiz generation

↓

Translation

↓

Interactive HTML lesson generation

↓

Teacher review

↓

Student publication

This significantly reduces manual effort while maintaining educator oversight.

---

# 7. Proposed Business Solution

AILPG provides a unified platform that:

- Accepts educational MP4 videos.
- Processes content using AI.
- Generates interactive learning materials.
- Allows teacher review before publishing.
- Delivers lessons through a modern student portal.
- Collects learning analytics for continuous improvement.

---

---

## For Institutions

- Reduced operating costs
- Faster content publishing

- Scalable course production

---

# 9. Success Indicators

The business problem is considered effectively addressed when:

- Course creation time is significantly reduced.
- Teacher approval of AI-generated lessons is consistently high.
- Student engagement increases through interactive content.
- Institutions can publish more courses with the same or fewer manual steps.

Specific targets are defined in `Success_Metrics.md`.
