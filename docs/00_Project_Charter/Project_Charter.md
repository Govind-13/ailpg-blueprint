# 1. Executive Summary

AILPG is an AI-powered SaaS platform that automatically converts educational MP4 videos into interactive HTML learning experiences.

The platform analyzes uploaded videos using Artificial Intelligence and automatically generates:

- Speech transcript
- Mathematical formula recognition
- OCR text extraction
- Topic segmentation
- Interactive quizzes
- Hints
- Explanations
- Multi-language translation
- Subtitles
- Interactive HTML lessons

The goal is to reduce manual course creation while increasing student engagement.

---

# 2. Background

Educational videos are widely used in schools, universities, coaching centers, and online learning platforms.

However, traditional MP4 videos are passive.

Teachers manually create:

- Quizzes
- Notes
- HTML pages
- Assignments
- Subtitles
- Multiple language versions

This process is time-consuming and expensive.

AILPG automates this workflow.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Current educational video platforms have several limitations:

- Passive video consumption
- Low student engagement
- Manual content preparation
- No automatic quiz generation
- No AI tutor
- Limited multilingual support
- Limited learning analytics

These limitations increase operational costs and reduce learning effectiveness.

---

# 4. Vision

Enable educators to upload a single MP4 video and automatically generate a complete AI-powered interactive learning experience.

---

# 5. Mission

To build a scalable AI platform that transforms traditional educational videos into intelligent, engaging, multilingual, and interactive learning content.

# 12. Assumptions

- Cloud infrastructure is available.
- GPU resources are available for AI processing.
- Internet connectivity is stable.
- Supported browsers are modern Chromium, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

---

# 13. Constraints

- AI processing depends on video duration.
- Large uploads require background processing.
- GPU availability affects processing speed.
- Third-party AI services may have usage limits.

---

# 14. Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| OCR inaccuracies | Medium | Human review workflow |
| Translation errors | Medium | Teacher review before publishing |
| GPU shortages | High | Queue processing and autoscaling |
| High storage cost | Medium | Lifecycle policies and compression |
| AI model failures | High | Retry strategy and monitoring |
