# Product Vision

> **Vision Statement**

Transform every educational video into an intelligent, interactive, multilingual learning experience powered by Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of AILPG is to eliminate the manual effort required to convert educational videos into complete digital learning courses.

Teachers should only upload an MP4 video.

The platform should automatically generate:

- Transcript
- Interactive questions
- Notes
- Subtitles
- Multi-language translations
- Interactive HTML lessons
- Student analytics

---

# Vision Statement

To become the leading AI-powered platform that enables educators worldwide to create interactive digital learning experiences from ordinary educational videos.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to reduce course development time while improving learning quality through AI automation.

---

# Core Philosophy

Learning should be:

- Interactive
- Personalized
- Accessible
- Multilingual
- Measurable
- AI-assisted

Passive video watching should become active learning.

---

# Product Principles

## 1. AI First

Every repetitive educational task should be automated whenever practical.

Examples:

- Speech transcription
- OCR
- Formula recognition
- Question generation
- Translation
- HTML generation

---

## 2. Human Review

AI generates content.

Teachers approve content.

The teacher remains responsible for the final published lesson.

---

## 3. Accessibility

The platform should be usable by:

- School students
- College students
- Competitive exam learners
- Teachers
- Coaching institutes

Support for accessibility features such as keyboard navigation, captions, and screen-reader compatibility should be considered.

---

## 4. Mobile First

Students should have a smooth experience on:

- Desktop
- Tablet
- Mobile

---

## 5. Scalable Architecture

The system should support growth from small classrooms to enterprise deployments.

Target scale:

- Thousands of teachers
- Millions of students
